Super solid. Nakaka ilnag kanin ako lagi pag eto order. Nakakamiss yung sisig ni Tita Cora noon sa Raymundo. Solid din yung sisig ng Tresye Syete guys.

Sinigang na Baboy

Not just any sinigang guys, specifically this one. At hindi lang kung ano-anong sinigang na baboy, but my dad's original recipe. Yung may gabi na halo tapos sobrang lapot. Sheesh sobrang sarap guys.

Milk Tea

Eto talaga guys, I can never get enough of this. Pag need ng pampalamig at sweets ng katawan ko, eto agad hanap ko. Syempre di pwedeng walang sinkers 'to cuz that's part of the experience!

Coffee

Of course to finish the list, we need coffee here. No to instant coffee tayo guys (pero pwede narin kung desperate talaga), dapat brewed. Sheeesh. Yung matapang tapos may onting tamis. Parang buhay, you gotta savor the bitterness to truly enjoy the sweetness. Wuw.
